About the Platform & Developer ▼

The Cyber Crime Investigation Portal is a secure, dedicated platform developed exclusively for use by Police and Law Enforcement Agencies (LEAs). It provides a centralized interface to access categorized Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) tools, bots, cyber investigation techniques, and reporting features — all designed to support real-time digital crime investigation efforts.

With features like secure login access, tool filtering, collapsible categories, dark mode, and report generation, the platform ensures operational security, usability, and efficiency for field investigators and cybercrime units.

This portal is developed by Vedant Hondre, a Junior Detective and Certified Ethical Hacker, currently serving with the Maharashtra Police. Vedant is also a passionate Cybersecurity Researcher focused on digital forensics, OSINT, and ethical hacking. His vision is to empower law enforcement teams through the use of reliable and modern cyber tools.

Through this platform, Vedant combines his investigative experience and technical skills to provide a trusted digital solution that bridges the gap between technology and enforcement in the fight against cybercrime.
